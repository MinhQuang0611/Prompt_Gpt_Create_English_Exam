Here are some exercises designed specifically for Vietnamese students at a beginner level (IELTS 1.0-3.0). The exercises focus on foundational language skills and provide clear, bilingual instructions.

### Exercise 1: Basic Vocabulary - Daily Activities

**Language Focus:** Basic Everyday Vocabulary

**Objective:** Learn and practice words related to daily activities.

**Instructions (Hướng dẫn):**
1. Look at the pictures below.
2. Read the words and match them to the pictures.
3. Practice saying each word aloud.

**Vocab List (Danh sách từ vựng):**
1. Eat (Ăn)
2. Sleep (Ngủ)
3. Study (Học)
4. Play (Chơi)
5. Work (Làm việc)

**Examples (Ví dụ):**
- Picture of a person eating (Hình ảnh một người đang ăn)
- Picture of a bed (Hình ảnh một chiếc giường)

**Activity (Hoạt động):**
- Match the words with the correct pictures (Ghép các từ với bức ảnh đúng)
- Say the words aloud three times (Đọc các từ to ba lần)

**Comprehension Check (Kiểm tra hiểu biết):**
- What do you do every day? (Bạn làm gì mỗi ngày?)
- How do you say "chơi" in English? (Bạn nói "chơi" bằng tiếng Anh là gì?)

---

### Exercise 2: Simple Present Tense - My Daily Routine

**Language Focus:** Simple Present Tense

**Objective:** Learn to form simple sentences using the simple present tense.

**Instructions (Hướng dẫn):**
1. Read the sentences below.
2. Fill in the blanks with the correct verb in the simple present tense.
3. Practice saying the sentences aloud.

**Examples (Ví dụ):**
- I (to eat) breakfast at 7:00 AM. → I eat breakfast at 7:00 AM.
- She (to study) English every day. → She studies English every day.

**Activity (Hoạt động):**
- I __________ (to sleep) at 10:00 PM.
- They __________ (to play) soccer after school.
- He __________ (to work) in a shop.

**Comprehension Check (Kiểm tra hiểu biết):**
- What time do you eat breakfast? (Bạn ăn sáng lúc mấy giờ?)
- How many hours do you sleep every night? (Bạn ngủ bao nhiêu giờ mỗi đêm?)

---

### Exercise 3: Writing - My Favorite Food

**Language Focus:** Basic Sentence Structures

**Objective:** Write simple sentences about your favorite food.

**Instructions (Hướng dẫn):**
1. Read the example sentences.
2. Write 2-3 sentences about your favorite food.

**Examples (Ví dụ):**
- My favorite food is rice. (Món ăn yêu thích của tôi là cơm.)
- I like pizza. (Tôi thích pizza.)

**Activity (Hoạt động):**
- __________ (My favorite food is _________.)
- __________ (I like _________.)
- __________ (I eat _________ on weekends.)

**Comprehension Check (Kiểm tra hiểu biết):**
- What is your favorite food? (Món ăn yêu thích của bạn là gì?)
- Can you describe it? (Bạn có thể mô tả nó không?)

---

### Exercise 4: Speaking - Self-Introduction

**Language Focus:** Essential Self-Introduction

**Objective:** Practice introducing yourself in English.

**Instructions (Hướng dẫn):**
1. Read the example introduction.
2. Practice your introduction with a partner.

**Example (Ví dụ):**
- Hello, my name is [Name]. (Xin chào, tên tôi là [Tên].)
- I am [age] years old. (Tôi [tuổi] tuổi.)
- I come from [City/Country]. (Tôi đến từ [Thành phố/Quốc gia].)

**Activity (Hoạt động):**
1. Hello, my name is ________.
2. I am ________ years old.
3. I come from ________.

**Comprehension Check (Kiểm tra hiểu biết):**
- What is your name? (Tên bạn là gì?)
- How old are you? (Bạn bao nhiêu tuổi?)

---

### Exercise 5: Listening - Short Dialogues

**Language Focus:** Elementary Listening Comprehension

**Objective:** Improve listening skills with short dialogues.

**Instructions (Hướng dẫn):**
1. Listen to the audio file (if available).
2. Answer the questions based on what you hear.

**Examples (Ví dụ):**
- A: What do you like to eat?
- B: I like noodles.

**Activity (Hoạt động):**
1. Listen to the conversation. (Nghe đoạn hội thoại.)
2. Answer: 
   - What does B like to eat? (B thích ăn gì?)
   - Do they like fruit? (Họ có thích trái cây không?)

**Comprehension Check (Kiểm tra hiểu biết):**
- What did you hear? (Bạn đã nghe gì?)
- Can you tell me the answer to the question? (Bạn có thể cho tôi biết đáp án không?)

---

These exercises will help Vietnamese students at the beginner level build their foundational English skills in vocabulary, grammar, listening, speaking, and writing. Make sure to provide plenty of support and practice opportunities as they develop their abilities.
